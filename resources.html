How can I increase my typing speed?

The best way to increase typing speed is to learn to type correctly using the method of “Touch typing” instead of using a “hunt and peck”.

     

What is Touch Typing?

Touch typing means you are able to type with all 10 fingers without looking at the keyboard. It involves using muscle memory to find keys quickly and accurately. Mastering touch typing can greatly enhance your typing speed and efficiency.

You can practice typing and building your speed by playing typing games, such as this one.
